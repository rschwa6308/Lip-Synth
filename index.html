Lip Synth

Open your mouth and hear the music! You can see what note is being played in the top left of the video. Try changing instruments and keys to find a sound you like! Having trouble holding notes, or is there too few notes for your liking? Use the slider adjusts the sensitivity of the instrument! Also feel free to toggle on the face tracking indicators to see how it works.
Want to try learning a song? Lip Synth can teach you! First press the login button and login to spotify. Type a song that you want to sing in the input field. Then press 'submit' to lock it in and press 'play song' when you're ready to start! Open your mouth to the size of the green ellipse to play along! The dashed ellipse shows you what your next note will be. Dont worry about changing key to match, when a song is selected, the key is automatically updated.
